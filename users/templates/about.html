about

welcome to aluminotes!

For students and professionals facing challenges in note-taking, Aluminotes provides an AI-powered solution to enhance text organization and lead to improvements in users' writings. Unlike baseline AI tools like ChatGPT, our software offers personalized formatting options with the ability to customize outputs to cater to user needs, setting a new standard for efficiency and quality in digital writing.

designed with you in mind

Aluminotes was formed from the shared struggle of students who experienced difficulties with writing and managing notes under tight schedules, and an understanding for peers who find their own writing assignments to be lacking. Aluminotes is made to aid students and career professionals who desire improvements in their notes, reports, articles, presentations, and more.

access and benefits

Aluminotes can be accessed immediately by any user with an account. Users who purchase a subscription plan will experience Aluminotes with all additional accessibility features, more feedback options, and access to community tools to develop and share their own note-taking templates!
